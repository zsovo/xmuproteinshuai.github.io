About the laboratory

Applying the concepts and methods of physics, informatics and biology, especially with the help of the new achievements of nonlinear dynamics, non-equilibrium statistical theory, bioinformatics and systems biology, the biological systems under health and pathological conditions are modeled at different levels of molecules, cells, tissues and systems. Numerical simulation and theoretical analysis are combined to quantitatively graduate students The relationship between structure and function, the transmission of energy and information in the process of life activities, and the control and treatment of biological system under pathological conditions. The main research directions include: cell signal network dynamics at cell level, neuron dynamics, calcium ion signal, cancer cell dynamics; immune system dynamics, biological neural network model and cancer cell invasion dynamics at the organization system level; and big data mining modeling based on health and medical big data.
